Chal Rahe Projects (Ongoing)

Duniya Badalne Mein Hamara Saath Dein

Hum garibi aur bhedbhav mitane ke kai kadam utha rahe hain. Dekhiye aapka donation kahan istemaal hota hai.

Shiksha

Village School Nirman

Ek gaon mein primary school banane mein madad karein. Yahan ke kareeb 300 bacche roz 10km paidal chalkar padhne jaate hain. Aapki madad inka samay aur padhai dono bachayegi.

Collected: ₹12,00,000 Target: ₹20,00,000
142 Madadgaar Donate Karein
Swasthya

Mobile Medical Clinic (Van)

Gareeb aur pichde ilako mein mobile medical van chalu karne ke liye fund dijiye. Isse asani se logon tak free ilaj, dawaiyan aur checkups pahunchega.

Collected: ₹8,00,000 Target: ₹15,00,000
89 Madadgaar Donate Karein
Poshan

Zero Hunger Pehal

Slums ke bacchon ko poshtik madhyahan bhojan (mid-day meals) pradan karein. Bohot se masoomo ke liye yeh din ka akela bhar-pet khana hota hai.

Collected: ₹4,00,000 Target: ₹10,00,000
215 Madadgaar Donate Karein
Paani (Water)

Saaf Peene Ka Paani

Sookhe aur pani ki kami wale gaon mein hand-pumps aur water wells lagwane mein madad karein, taki waha beemariyan na phaile.

Collected: ₹15,00,000 Target: ₹18,00,000
320 Madadgaar Donate Karein
Anaathashram (Orphanage)

Daya Orphanage Vistar

Hamare naye anaathashram mein 50 aur aanaath bacchon ko panah dene ke liye ashiyana aur bed banwane main funds ka yogdaan karein.

Collected: ₹7,00,000 Target: ₹25,00,000
95 Madadgaar Donate Karein
